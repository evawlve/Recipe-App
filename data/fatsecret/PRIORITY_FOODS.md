## Category Recommendations

### New Categories to Add
1. **Dairy – Low-Fat & Fat-Free** (`dairy:low-fat`)
   - All fat-free and reduced-fat dairy items
   
2. **Pantry – Keto Alternatives** (`pantry:keto`)
   - Keto breads, tortillas, baking ingredients
   
3. **Pantry – Canned Goods** (`pantry:canned`)
   - Canned vegetables, beans, proteins, broths
   
4. **Protein – Supplements** (`protein:supplements`)
   - Protein powders, bars, meal replacements
   
5. **Pantry – Nut Butters** (`pantry:nut-butters`)
   - All nut and seed butters (separate from nuts/seeds)

### Categories to Expand
- `pantry:grains` - Add more breads, wraps, pasta varieties
- `pantry:condiments` - Add more vinegars, sauces
- `protein:seafood` - Add more frozen and canned options
- `beverages` - Add more plant milks, teas, functional beverages
- `produce:vegetables` - Add more herbs and aromatics

## Implementation Strategy

1. **Phase 1: Health-Focused (Week 1)**
   - Fat-free and reduced-fat cheeses (20-30 items)
   - Keto breads and tortillas (10-15 items)
   - Keto baking ingredients (5-10 items)

2. **Phase 2: Protein Supplements (Week 2)**
   - Protein powders (10-15 items)
   - Protein bars (10-15 items)
   - High-protein alternatives (5-10 items)

3. **Phase 3: Pantry Staples (Week 3)**
   - Canned goods (20-30 items)
   - Breads and wraps (15-20 items)
   - Nut butters and seeds (10-15 items)

4. **Phase 4: Fill Gaps (Week 4)**
   - Processed proteins (10-15 items)
   - Frozen seafood (5-10 items)
   - Plant milks (5-10 items)
   - Herbs and spices (10-15 items)

## Notes

- Focus on **branded items** when available (better accuracy)
- Include **store brands** (Kroger, Aldi, Walmart) for common items
- Prioritize **low-sodium** versions when available
- For cheeses, include both **shredded** and **block** varieties
- For breads, include both **regular** and **keto/low-carb** versions
- Test search terms in FatSecret API to find best matches
